HERO STYLE OPTIONS
------------------
The site ships with Version A (clean blue gradient hero) as the default.
Version B (lab-tech PHOTO behind the gradient) is included as style_B.css.

To switch to the PHOTO hero (Version B):
  - Rename assets/style.css      -> assets/style_A.css   (backup)
  - Rename assets/style_B.css    -> assets/style.css
Commit & push. That's the only change needed.
