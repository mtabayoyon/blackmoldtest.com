Why Is Humidity Control Equipment a Smart Investment for Homeowners? Insights about Home Dampness Prevention

2016-12-31 · National Indoor Environmental Testing

Most homeowners are reluctant to purchase a dehumidifier until it is absolutely necessary. However, many are surprised to learn that they can actually save money in the long-run by installing a home dampness prevention kit.

It’s no secret that high humidity levels can make your living space uncomfortable. And if there are people in your home who struggle with hair frizz, they will face an uphill climb if your indoor air is humid.

In addition to making your home a more enjoyable place to live, dehumidifiers can work for your wallet by preventing damage to your wooden floors and furniture, protecting your family from mold-related health complications, and safeguarding your home from structural damage.

Let’s take a closer look at three reasons why a home mold control system is a smart investment:

1. Prevents Damage to Your Wooden Floors and Furniture


When exposed to high humidity levels, wood tends to weaken and bend. That translates into creaking floors and wooden furniture that is susceptible to damage.

A high-end home dehumidifier will set you back a few hundred dollars, but replacing wooden floors or furniture can cost thousands. You can prevent this expense by installing a home dampness prevention unit.

2. Protects Your Family from Mold-Related Health Complications 


When was the last time you went to the doctor? Chances are, you were addressing a potentially serious health concern for yourself or a family member because treating a minor ailment is not worth the cost of a medical evaluation.

One of the primary reasons why homeowners purchase dehumidifiers is to prevent mold growth. Mold releases spores into the air. When inhaled, these spores can cause potentially fatal allergic reactions, asthma attacks and respiratory illnesses, according to the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. Home dehumidifiers can prevent these complications and save you the exorbitant cost of doctors’ visits.

3. Prevents Structural Damage to Your Home 


Mold can damage your walls to a point where, if left unaddressed, the restoration cost could reach several thousand dollars. Dehumidifiers prevent mold outbreaks and, as a result, save you the expense of repairing your home after a mold infestation.
