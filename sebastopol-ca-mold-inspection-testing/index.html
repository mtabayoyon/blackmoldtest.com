Sebastopol Mold Inspection & Testing

Black mold and hidden moisture problems are a serious concern for Sebastopol homeowners and businesses. National Indoor Environmental Testing (NIET) provides certified mold inspection, air quality testing, and lab analysis throughout Sebastopol and Sonoma County. Call 855-349-2356 to schedule.

Professional Mold Inspection in Sebastopol

Sebastopol's cool, damp west-county climate and its agricultural setting among apple orchards and vineyards create ideal conditions for indoor mold. Coastal fog reaches inland here, keeping humidity high through much of the year.

Local Housing & Why Mold Grows Here

Sebastopol features older farmhouses, craftsman homes, and rural properties on acreage, along with newer in-town housing. Agricultural outbuildings, barns, and older homes with limited ventilation are especially prone to moisture problems.

Common Sebastopol mold triggers: coastal fog and west-county humidity, seasonal creek flooding, moisture in agricultural buildings and older farmhouses, and crawl spaces that stay damp through the rainy season.

Our Sebastopol Mold Testing Process

A certified NIET inspector conducts a thorough visual and moisture assessment using industry-standard equipment, including moisture meters, thermal imaging, and air and surface sampling. Samples are analyzed by an accredited lab, and you receive a clear written report identifying any mold species, spore counts, and the moisture source driving growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a mold inspection in Sebastopol cost?

Mold inspection pricing depends on property size and the number of samples needed. We provide a clear quote before any work begins, and testing includes lab analysis and a written report. Call 855-349-2356.

What's the difference between mold testing and mold inspection?

A mold inspection is a visual and moisture assessment to locate growth and its source. Mold testing adds air or surface samples sent to an accredited lab to identify species and spore counts. Call 855-349-2356.

Do I need mold testing before buying or selling a home in Sebastopol?

It's strongly recommended. Given Sebastopol's housing and moisture conditions, a pre-purchase mold inspection protects buyers and helps sellers disclose accurately. Call 855-349-2356.

Is black mold in Sebastopol dangerous?

Black mold (Stachybotrys) can release mycotoxins linked to respiratory and other health issues. If you see or smell mold, avoid disturbing it and schedule a professional inspection. Call 855-349-2356.
